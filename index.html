Selected work

I studied literature and philosophy before I studied data. I don't think of these as different phases of a person — they're the same practice: learning to ask what something actually means, rather than just what it says.

Right now I'm finishing an MSc in Data Science & AI Strategy at emlyon, on exchange at TU Berlin, and interning in compliance — helping large companies understand what the law is genuinely asking of them, not just how to satisfy it on paper.

The through-line, if there is one: I'm interested in the places where language becomes consequential.
